Cinnamon Toast Cereal

Don't worry, this one is easy!

You will need the following

  • A bowl of your choice
  • About a cup of milk
  • Cinnamon Toast cereal
  • A spoon

Follow the next steps and you should be all good to go!

  1. Put the bowl on a hard surface, then put the spoon in the bowl

  2. Pour your desired amount of cereal into the bowl

  3. Fill the bowl with milk until the cereal is barely above the top of the bowl

  4. Enjoy!
