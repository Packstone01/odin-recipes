Late Night Recipes

Some of the BEST and EASIEST recipes are the ones that first come to mind. Here are some recipes you can enjoy in the light of day or the shade of night. Whenever it is, sit back, relax and enjoy the eats!

Cinnamon Toast Cereal

A classic recipe is the gold 'ole bowl of cereal. Whether you like crispy Cinnamon Toast or fluffy Reese's Puff, a bowl of cereal is a classic midnight snack.

Tacos

Another well known and very popular dish is the Taco. This can be as simple as a ground beef and some cheese, or some Al Pastor tacos with the works. Both are great for a family get together or a Skyrim 6 launch.
